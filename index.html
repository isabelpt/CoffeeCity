Coffee City: An analysis of New York City coffee shops

By Isabel Prado-Tucker

I analyzed NYC Restaurant Inspection Data to investigate the quality, cleanliness, and density of coffee shops. The first figure maps out coffee shops on the neighborhood level, and the second figure compares chain vs. indie coffee shops. Click one of the buttons to get started!
